About Me

Bridging research & real-world semiconductor technology

I'm Yoshiki Takeuchi, an electrical engineering graduate from Purdue University, specializing in semiconductor research and cleanroom fabrication. I'll be pursuing a master's degree at UC Berkeley, focusing on semiconductor and thin film.

My recent internships at TSMC and Micron involved process integration and advanced packaging, where I honed my skills in cutting-edge semiconductor technologies.
